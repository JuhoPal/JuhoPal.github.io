Who are we

We at GreenTools aspire to be everyone's economic and ecologic companion. We champion the sharing economy and live according to a simple rule. It is far better to share the tools you own instead of owning more tools. Green Tools exists because life is more than just about owning more things.

The business idea sproutet in 2017 from a rather practical problem. Moving to suburbs brought a house and a yeard to take care of, and a number of other things to consider. As a new home owner, there was a lawn mover to be purchased, circular saw for building a patio, wheelbarrows to move the soil, concrete mixing tools, trash bins and many others. Next thing you know is that you need a toolshed for all the things you own. More space was required and the dream of a small house with a compact gardgin begun to materialize quite literally.

It took few years to refine the business model but around 2023 at the Turku University of Applied Sciences' business course ideas begun to collide and take shape. What was first a half serious project slowly turned into actionable plans. GreenTools ltd. was founded in 2024.

We at GreenTools are convinced that the current Mega Trends will shape economy through circular economy and distribution of agencies. Circualr economy will be vital for everyone from gen-Z and beyond. Circular economy will become a commong way to generate wealth. Ecological business becomes a cornerstone in which everyone can join.

We know that we are a small player in the vast sea of environmentally conscious businesses. But one thing is for sure. Every small step towards the freedom to choose nature rather than wasting its resources is worth taking. It will make the next generations stronger and the world a better place to live.
